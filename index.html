The Land of Promise

Top three activities to do when you visit Uyo.

Relax at Le Meridien Hotel & Golf Resort

Inspired by the era of glamorous travel, Le Meridien unlocks destinations around the world, celebrating each culture through the distinctly European spirit.

Enjoy a Delicious plate of Afang Soup
Afang soup, native to the Efik and Ibibio people of Nigeria is a delicious Calabar vegetable soup made with Afang leaves and a vegetable called waterleaf.

Have fun at Ibom Tropicana Center

A huge glass elevation, the welcoming entrance & all high quality provided services make it a must visit place where comfort and relaxation reside.
